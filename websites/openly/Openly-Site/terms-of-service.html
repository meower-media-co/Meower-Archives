Terms of Service (updated November 12, 2020)

Section 1: Introduction

Welcome to Openly! We hope you enjoy our service, with love from the Openly Team. However, to enjoy Openly, there are some rules you must follow, and breaking these rules may result in moderation action being taken against your account (and the possible disruption of others' Openly experience).

Section 2: Warning Points

Once you violate the Terms of Service (hereinafter "ToS"), Warning Points will be added to your account.

Warning Points are given on a scale of 1-10 depending on the severity of the rule.

  1. Extremely Minor Violation
  2. Very Minor Violation
  3. Minor Violation
  4. Slightly Moderate Violation
  5. Moderate Violation
  6. Intermediate Violation
  7. High Violation
  8. Higher Violation
  9. Final Warning
  10. Unacceptable Violation

Breaking a rule that earns more than 3 Warning Points will result in a personal warning from a moderator.

Getting 10 Warning Points in total on your account will result in you getting banned for a certain period of time, and getting more than 10 Warning Points will result in a permanent ban (hereinafter "termination") on your account and under your IP address.

Being banned does not reset your Warning Points, so another broken rule would result in a termination.

Section 3: Rules

Age Restriction

When signing up to or continuing to use Openly, you are automatically assumed to be over 13. Being under 13 and using our file sharing or browsing services constitutes a violation of this rule. Under 18s (or your state of residence's legal age of majority) must have adult permission and be linked to a parent email to access our site.

10+ Warning Points

Ban Evasion

Using an alternate account to evade a ban or termination will result in termination of the alternate account. Only a maximum of 3 accounts under a single IP address are allowed.

10+ Warning Points

Inappropriate Content

There are many things that are considered inappropriate content here.

  • Harrassment towards other Openers
  • Content that is not appropriate for anybody 13+
  • Pornographic or sexual content
  • Discrimination towards people of different races, sexual orientations, backgrounds, skin colors, etc.
3+ or more Warning Points

Code

Using code for inappropriate use or hacking is not allowed on Openly. Examples fo bad code:

  • Infested C++ or Javascript
  • JS, C++, C#, C+, jQuery, etc that hacks and gets personal info, passwords, etc
  • Inappropriate links or embeds
  • Code that keeps you on the same tab (You close the tab but then it pops back up again)
6+ or more Warning Points
